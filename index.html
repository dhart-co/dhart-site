Our Portfolio

Take a look at some of our works and projects.

Humanify

Product

Humanify is an innovative HR system. With AI based sentiment analysis, collaborators registers, chatbots and web scrapping, we give your HR team a clear vision of what your company is doing wrong and how to improve it.

Essay Corrector

Product

We developed an innovative mobile App that automatically evaluates ENEM essays made by students. By taking a photo of their handwritten essay, they get a complete evaluation of their work, with feedbacks and ratings. Made for EstudoPlay, the system is already deployed and being used by thousands of students in Brazil.

Dhartbet

Product

Dhartbet is a data science and analysis tool focused on gathering insights on different data on the esports betting world, allowing the user to make smarter choices when betting.

Monitoria

Product

Monitoria is a scheduling and organizational tool aimed to allowing schools to see and receives insights on how the tutors are perfoming when teaching their students.

Dock Innovation Lab

Competition by Dock

Won first place in a Hackathon sponsored by Dock. The competiton had the goal of creating a tool to solve the climate research problem inside the HR sector of the company. Our solution represented a full HR system for any company to use.

Hack The Music

Competition by Moises

Won first place in a Hackathon sponsored by Moises and Music.ai. The compatition aimed at creating a new tool with the company's services and APIs. We developed a Google Extension that allows the user to utilize their main tools directly in a Youtube video.
